Biography

I am currently an Assistant Professor in the College of Artificial Intelligence, National University of Defense Technology (NUDT).

My research focuses on Task Planning, Motion Planning and Prediction for intelligent vehicles. Recently, I am working on learning-based motion planning and interpretable decision-making.
